About Eggcellent

Eggcellent is a cozy breakfast-and-boba spot located along Western Ave, popular with students for its creative waffle dishes, breakfast sandwiches, and customizable bubble tea drinks. The atmosphere is bright and modern with fast service and affordable prices.
